Preview & Publish

Preview your project before you publish to see the final result

Preview

Click the Preview button in the top right corner of your window
This will open up a new tab where you can see a full preview of your current page/component

Publish

Click on the Publish button in the top right corner to see the deploy options
You can choose to publish through TeleportHQ on one of our subdomains
If you want to change the subdomain go to the Project Settings - General from the menu
Or you can opt for a custom deploy, using your preferred domain, by setting up a Vercel token
To add a Vercel token go to Project Settings - Tokens
